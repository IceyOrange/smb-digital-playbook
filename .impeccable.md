## Design Context

### Users
Small business owners and operators (coffee shops, salons, gyms, boutiques, clinics, etc.) who want to see how other real shops digitized their workflows. They browse the playbook on desktop and mobile, looking for a scenario that matches their own, then copy a prompt to generate their own mini-app on whacka. They are non-technical and value clarity, authenticity, and speed.

### Brand Personality
- **Real**: Every case is pulled from an actual shop-floor scenario, not abstract software features.
- **Warm**: Friendly, approachable, and human — no enterprise SaaS coldness.
- **Practical**: Show the setup, explain the result, give the prompt. No code, no buzzwords.

Voice: plain-spoken, confident, helpful. Emotional goal: "If they can do it, so can I."

### Aesthetic Direction
A light, warm, screenshot-first showcase. The App screenshots are the heroes; decoration should get out of the way. The layout should feel like a curated wall of real phone interfaces rather than a generic card grid. Avoid heavy shadows, gradient backgrounds behind screenshots, and templated phone frames unless consistently applied.

- **Theme**: light mode
- **Palette**: warm off-white surfaces (`#fcf9f3`), warm brown text (`#2a2118`), terracotta accent (`#c45d2b`), muted secondary text (`#6b5e50`)
- **Typography**: `Libre Baskerville` for display headings, system sans for body
- **Anti-references**: cyan-on-dark SaaS dashboards, heavy glassmorphism, generic drop-shadow cards, gradient text, identical icon+heading+text card grids

### Design Principles
1. **Screenshots first**: The App mockups are the proof. Give them space and a quiet background so they communicate clearly.
2. **Consistent proportions**: All screenshots are phone-shaped; present them with a uniform aspect ratio and scale so the grid feels orderly.
3. **Minimal decorative noise**: No competing gradients, heavy shadows, or decorative frames behind screenshots.
4. **Performance is part of the design**: Lazy-load the 25 screenshots so the page feels fast.
5. **Responsive, not amputated**: Adapt the card layout for mobile without cropping the screenshot or hiding actions.
