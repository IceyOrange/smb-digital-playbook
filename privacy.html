Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 7, 2026

What we collect

The Small Business Playbook does not ask visitors to log in, subscribe, or submit forms. We do not collect email addresses, names, phone numbers, or any other personally identifiable information.

We use PostHog to collect anonymous usage data — such as page views, scroll depth, time on page, and button clicks — so we can improve the site. PostHog's default configuration may also capture device and IP-related data in an aggregated, non-identifying way. Session replay is not enabled.

How we use data

Analytics data is used only to understand how people use the playbook and to improve the content. We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties because we do not collect personal data.

External links

The site links to live app examples hosted on whacka.app. Those apps have their own privacy policies and terms of service.

Cookies

PostHog may use first-party cookies to distinguish anonymous sessions. You can disable cookies in your browser if you prefer.
