Common questions

What is the Small Business Playbook?

The Small Business Playbook is a showcase of real-world digital setups for small businesses. Each case includes the original problem, a copy-ready prompt, and a live app example built with whacka.

Do I need to code to use these setups?

No coding is required. You can copy the English prompt from any case, paste it into whacka.app, and get a working mini-app tailored to your shop.

Which industries are covered?

The playbook covers Food & Beverage, Retail, Fitness, Beauty, Education, Healthcare, Services, Pet Care, Home Services, and Creative businesses.

Can I customize the app for my own business?

Yes. Each setup is a starting point. After generating the app on whacka, you can change the name, colors, items, and workflow to match your exact business needs.

How is this different from Shopify, Square, or hiring a developer?

Unlike general website builders, each playbook setup is a ready-to-run mini-app shaped for a specific shop workflow. You do not write code or manage plugins. Copy the prompt into whacka, answer a few questions, and get a working app in minutes.

How much does it cost to build one of these apps?

The playbook is free to browse. Building on whacka has a free tier for experiments; paid tiers are typically a small monthly subscription. For most small shops, the total cost is far below custom development.

Can I try a setup before using it for my real business?

Yes. Every case links to a live app example. Open it, tap through the workflow, and when you are ready, copy the prompt to generate your own version with your name, items, prices, and hours.

Your shop deserves a setup that actually fits

Coffee shop, nail studio, gym, convenience store — it doesn't matter. Pick one case and try it. The cost of experimenting is basically zero.
